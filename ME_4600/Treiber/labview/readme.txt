Windows:
========
- LabVIEW 5: Unzip the self-extracting archive "me4600_lv50.exe" 
  to a directory of your choice (default: "C:\Meilhaus\Me-4600\labview").
 
- LabVIEW 6i and higher: Unzip the self-extracting archive 
  "me4600_lv.exe" to a directory of your choice 
  (default: "C:\Meilhaus\Me-4600\labview").


Linux:
======
LabVIEW support for Linux: Unzip the archive "me4000_lv_linux.tar.gz"
